# How I Came to Exhibit at Vket

Most of what I make at work can't be shared publicly,\
so outside of work, I've always kept practicing and making different things in my own time.

Partly to keep building up a body of work,\
and partly because I want to slowly create things I genuinely love—things that feel like me.

Then, as chance would have it, I enrolled in a character course at [Kimina Studio](https://kimina-studio.com/),\
and that's when I first saw the senior students talking about Vket 2025 Winter.

At the time, I had absolutely no idea what Vket was.

After a quick, sneaky Google search, I learned that it was a large-scale virtual exhibition held inside VRChat—\
like bringing a doujin convention, a creators' market, and an exhibition into a virtual world you can actually step inside and explore.

After that, I asked a friend to teach me how to play VRChat.\
We visited all kinds of places, went to a dance club, and toured Vket 2025 Winter with our friends.

At the Vket venue, I even came across [Tsuyuna](https://booth.pm/ja/items/7506277), one of my absolute favorites.\
When New Year's came around, I even got to watch the fireworks with everyone in VRChat.

At that moment, a strange feeling suddenly came over me.

I realized that something someone creates doesn't have to just sit there on "display."\
It can also become a place people actually step into, spend time in, and even make memories with their friends.

That was the first time I had a thought like this:\
"Maybe someday, I'd like to create a place like that myself—a place where people can step inside, spend time together, and make memories with their friends."

## Why I Wanted to Exhibit at Vket

After finishing my desktop pet mini-game, life suddenly got very busy.

Besides work suddenly becoming much busier,\
changes in the world, ever-rising prices, and anxiety about the future made me rethink how I wanted to work going forward.

After checking with my company, I started taking on small commissions after hours.\
And that's exactly when I saw the applications open for Vket 2026 Summer.

Honestly, I didn't think too much about it at the time. XD"\
~~Inner voice: I probably... won't get picked, right...?~~ `ˊ_>ˋ`

Considering my work schedule, I didn't apply for the more demanding Space category,\
and only applied for the Item category.

If I get picked, I'll make it. If not, let's pretend this never happened. XD"

...~~Sometimes you really shouldn't tempt fate~~...\
How did I actually get picked? Orz

And so, this application—sent off while thinking, "I probably won't get picked, right?"—\
unexpectedly became the starting point of my very first Vket.

I panicked a little at first and was afraid I wouldn't be able to finish,\
so I didn't dare tell anyone at all!

But since I really did get picked, I decided not to overthink it and to try as many of the things I wanted to do as possible.

Getting picked unexpectedly also gave me a push at just the right time.

It gave me the chance to try making something that wouldn't just sit there on display,\
but might be discovered by someone, played with, and eventually become part of one of their VRChat memories.
