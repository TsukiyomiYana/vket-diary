# Vket Dashboard Notes

## Preview World (下見ワールド)

Under **入稿 (Submission) → 入稿 / 修正 (Submission / Edit) → 入稿方法 (Submission Method)**,\
you'll find the **下見ワールドへ (Enter the Preview World)** button.

Clicking it first shows the **入場規約 (Entry Terms for the Preview World)**.\
Read and agree to them, and you can enter the Preview World to check how your item looks and behaves in the actual venue.

Do read the entry terms carefully.\
If you violate the terms, your **eligibility to exhibit may be revoked**. Depending on the extent of the damage caused, you may even be held **legally liable**.
