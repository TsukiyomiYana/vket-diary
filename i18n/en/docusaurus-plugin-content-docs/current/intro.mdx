# Vket 2026 Summer Exhibition Diary

This is a record of my first time exhibiting at Vket (Virtual Market).

So future me won't forget how I did all of this yet again,\
I'm going to properly, seriously, and as thoroughly as possible document the entire process, from preparation to the exhibition itself.

It includes ~~a whole lot of rambling~~, the exhibition and production process, and the problems I ran into along the way and how I solved them.

I hope this record can help and encourage more creators who also want to exhibit at Vket.

> ※ This is not an official tutorial—just a personal account and exhibition record.\
> ※ I didn't plan to keep a record when I first started, so most of the images were found or retaken afterward. Some parts may be incomplete—thank you for understanding.
> ※ The Japanese and English versions were translated with help from Claude. I'll give both versions a more thorough polish when I have time in the future.
