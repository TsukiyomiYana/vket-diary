# The Birth of the Stinky Tofu Sea Slug

The world I was assigned through the lottery was "[幻想コロニー コクーン水族美術館 Redive - パルス](https://vket.com/2026Summer/world/29)".

At first, I thought, "An aquarium museum, huh...\
Maybe I could make a little something related to the world's setting."

With my limited imagination,\
I spent a while trying to choose between a frog, a dolphin, a ray, and a sea slug.

When I casually searched online, I somehow ended up collecting a huge pile of sea slug reference images,\
yet I hadn't saved a single image for any of the other candidates.

...Uh. I think the answer was pretty obvious at that point. XD"

<StepGallery arrows={false} maxWidth="600px" steps={[
  { img: '/vket-diary/img/web-014.webp', label: 'Umiushi Reference' }
]} />

So in the end, I decided to go with the sea slug.\
Once the direction was settled, I drew a few sketches with different sea slug designs.

<StepGallery arrows={false} maxWidth="600px" steps={[
  { img: '/vket-diary/img/web-015.webp', label: 'Umiushi Sketch' }
]} />

As I kept drawing, I suddenly thought they looked a bit like little babies and a bit like certain foods.\
~~Absolutely not because I was sleepy and hungry at the time or anything...~~

So I drew a baby sea slug, a honey cake sea slug, and a pillow sea slug.

<StepGallery arrows={false} maxWidth="600px" steps={[
  { img: '/vket-diary/img/web-016.webp', label: 'Baby Umiushi / Food Umiushi' }
]} />

Then I remembered the mala stinky tofu from a few years ago, when I could still eat it,\
and how good that black stinky tofu was...

~~This person's brain usually isn't quite right while drawing.~~ `( °ヮ° ) ?`

After showing the sketches to my partner and a few friends,\
most people seemed to prefer the baby sea slug.

I originally thought, "In that case, I'll make the baby sea slug."

But even after the baby sea slug model was completely finished,\
I still had a soft spot for the black stinky tofu sea slug.

~~Stinky tofu really is delicious~~.

So in the end, I made a sudden last-minute U-turn\
and decided to follow my own preference and make the stinky tofu sea slug.

And that's how the Stinky Tofu Sea Slug was born.
