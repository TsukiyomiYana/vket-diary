# Vket Application & Lottery

Here's a quick record of what I did before applying to exhibit at Vket.

First, you'll need to create a Vket Account on the official [Vket](https://vket.com/) website.\
Second, since Vket itself is held inside VRChat, you'll need a VRChat account later to check your display, test it, and visit the event itself.\
So I'd also recommend setting up a [VRChat](https://hello.vrchat.com/) account in advance.

## Creating a Circle (サークル)

Once your account is ready,\
click "プロフィール (User Profile)" in the side menu and edit your profile.

<StepGallery arrows={false} maxWidth="480px" steps={[
  { img: '/vket-diary/img/web-003.webp', label: 'User Profile' }
]} />

Then go to "サークル管理 (Exhibitor Circles)" and select "新規登録 (Create a new circle)".\
Fill in your Circle information and submit it, and that's it—your Circle is ready.

<StepGallery steps={[
{ img: '/vket-diary/img/web-004.webp', label: 'Create a New Circle' },
{ img: '/vket-diary/img/web-005.webp', label: 'Enter Circle Information' },
]} />

If you're exhibiting with teammates, you can find "メンバー管理 (Edit Members)" on your Circle page.\
Select "メンバー追加 (Add Member)", enter your teammate's Vket ID, and send the invitation.\
Afterward, your teammate needs to accept the invitation from their own "サークル管理" page.\
Once that's done, they're officially part of your Circle.

**You must create a Circle before applying to exhibit, so this step is not optional.**

## Exhibition Application

Once your Circle is set up, head to "出展申し込み (Exhibition Application)" to apply.\
The application page offers different exhibition areas and formats. Choose the area and format you want, then submit your application.

<StepGallery steps={[
{ img: '/vket-diary/img/web-006.webp', label: 'Exhibition Application' },
{ img: '/vket-diary/img/web-007.webp', label: 'Application Submitted' },
]} />

If you're not sure which area to choose—~~but still want to improve your chances in the lottery~~—\
you can check the "出展申し込み中間発表" (mid-application status report) posted by [Vket's official account](https://x.com/Virtual_Market_) on X during the application period.\
Take a quick look at the current application status for each area, then decide whether to adjust your choices.

<StepGallery arrows={false} maxWidth="480px" steps={[
  { img: '/vket-diary/img/web-008.webp', label: '出展申し込み中間発表' }
]} />

> ~~※ The situation may be different for each event, so always follow the official announcements for the current event.~~\
~~※ There's an urban legend that the lottery mainly considers your 1st Choice, so... choose your 1st Choice carefully (superstition).~~

Pick your area and format, submit your application, and then it's time to wait for the lottery results.\
There are 2 lottery rounds in total.
