# Production Schedule

When I decided to join Vket,\
I already had—on top of my day job—a Live2D commission scheduled to wrap up in early May,\
and a long-term 3D commission scheduled to start at the end of May.

The submission window for Vket 2026 Summer was May 7 – June 15, 10:59 EDT.

The gap between wrapping up the Live2D job and starting the long-term 3D one\
was pretty much my only window to focus on Vket production.

If I could finish most of it before the end of May,\
the rest should just be adjustments, testing, and submission alongside the new commission.

......Right? `(ㆆᴗㆆ)`

I originally thought that once the Vket submission version was done,\
a little tidying up of the product files would be about it.

Then, halfway through the BOOTH prep, I spontaneously decided to add an Avatar version.

And while organizing the World version's Sit Prefab,\
a thought hit me: "Wouldn't it be more fun if you could switch between different sitting poses?"

So I started researching pose switching, writing scripts, building a menu, making 8 custom sitting poses,\
and even wrote a shader to keep the menu from showing up in the VRChat camera view...

And then, on top of all that, I went and built an exhibition space where people could actually try it out...Orz

At what point did I start getting so out of control? `(›´ω‹ )`
